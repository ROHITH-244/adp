RESUME RANKING

Our project, Resume Ranking, proposes a novel data-driven framework for personnel selection, integrating Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM) to analyze resumes and objectively rank candidates based on key skills and job requirements.

In today's competitive job market, it is essential for companies to efficiently evaluate numerous applicants. Our approach leverages advanced algorithms to process and understand the content of resumes, ensuring that the most qualified candidates are identified and recommended for interviews.
